About Me

Hi! I'm Anonymouse Whiskers, a private eye who roams the streets solving crime in Mousetropolis.
You may know me from my work on the Case of the Missing Cheese, or from my bestselling book Woe is Whiskers: The Tail of a Sleuth (2023).
While I live in Mousetropolis, I'm originally from Mouseville, a small town out west. I moved to Mousetropolis at 34 and never looked back!

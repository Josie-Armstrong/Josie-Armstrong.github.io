Goals

As a mouse detective, my greatest goal is to solve the Great Mousetropolis Heist of 1982.
While I was just a young mouse then, I've thought about it every day since. It's the greatest mystery of our time.
As a bestselling author of Woe is Whiskers: The Tail of a Sleuth, I want to continue writing the adventures of Arnold Whiskers.
Arnold, of course, has no relation to me (Anonymouse Whiskers), but his stories are inspired by my own sleuthing experiences.

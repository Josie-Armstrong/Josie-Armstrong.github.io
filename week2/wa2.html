About Me

Hi! I'm Anonymouse Whiskers, a private eye who roams the streets solving crime in Mousetropolis.
You may know me from my work on the Case of the Missing Cheese, or from my bestselling book Woe is Whiskers: The Tail of a Sleuth (2023).
While I live in Mousetropolis, I'm originally from Mouseville, a small town out west. I moved to Mousetropolis at 34 and never looked back!

Interests & Passions

  • Solving whisker-raising mysteries in Mousetropolis
  • Tipping my hat low mysteriously under a street light while it rains at night
  • Internal monologuing while I stand mysteriously under a street light at night
  • Writing bestselling mystery novels featuring an original character who bears a curious resemblance to yours truly
  • Eating cheese, of course

Goals

As a mouse detective, my greatest goal is to solve the Great Mousetropolis Heist of 1982.
While I was just a young mouse then, I've thought about it every day since. It's the greatest mystery of our time.
As a bestselling author of Woe is Whiskers: The Tail of a Sleuth, I want to continue writing the adventures of Arnold Whiskers.
Arnold, of course, has no relation to me (Anonymouse Whiskers), but his stories are inspired by my own sleuthing experiences.

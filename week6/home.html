Read

You may have some questions about certain characters, strategies, or rules as you start watching and/or playing. Blood on the Clocktower has its own wiki, so no need to worry! An abundance of information is at your fingertips.

You can access the wiki by clicking here. If you're looking for characters specific to Trouble Brewing, the script that's best for beginners, you can find a list of all Townsfolk, Outsiders, Minions, and Demons (there is only one on Trouble Brewing) right here, along with a quick synopsis and explanation of gameplay. Each character's page includes info on what they do, how that role is run, and even some strategy tips for both playing as and bluffing as (pretending to be) that character!

Play

You've watched videos, you've glanced at the wiki, and now you want to get your hands on a script and start playing! There are two ways to play: online, and in-person with the Boulder Clocktower group!

Online Play

You can play online using The Pandemonium Institute's official Clocktower web app. Once you've created an account and logged in, you can pick a lobby to play in and join! When you're starting out, look for games of Trouble Brewing that are marked as "Difficulty: Beginner." Those lobbies will be the most beginner-friendly, and are often playing with the goal of getting beginners into the game! As a note, you may have to wait for a game to finish before you can join.

In-Person Play

You can also play in-person with the Boulder Clocktower group (we would love to have you!). To view our upcoming events, visit the events page or join our Discord! You can request a link to join on the contact page.
